Key takeaways
What does this research tell us about RL in market making?

Tail-risk champion
SAC achieves superior CVaR under stress, protecting against catastrophic losses.
⚙️
Reward scaling is critical
Without normalization, RL agents exploit simulator artifacts, not market dynamics.
📊
Not a PnL maximizer
RL's edge is adaptive risk management, not beating heuristics on average returns.
